What I do

I simplify real estate transactions for international buyers and sellers in Cascais, Lisbon, and Sintra. I handle all documentation, legal, and financial aspects personally, ensuring a seamless journey from the initial search to the keys in your hands.

Who I am

A local, by your side.

My journey began in the competitive fashion industry in Israel, where I worked as a professional Buyer, learning to read quality, analyse market data and negotiate hard. In that world, "no" was always just the start of a deal.

Since moving to Cascais with my family in 2019, I have spent years immersed in the culture, the schools and the property market of the Golden Triangle. Today I represent both buyers and sellers, with the same sharp eye for value on either side.

My story

A clear-eyed read on value, and hard negotiation on your behalf.

Buying or selling in Portugal rewards local knowledge. I bring it to every deal, so the numbers make sense and nothing surfaces after you sign.
