My story

A local, by your side.

I'm Natalie Gutman. I've lived in Cascais since 2019, and I help international buyers find homes across the Golden Triangle, Lisbon, Cascais and Sintra, from first viewing to keys in hand, with the documentation, financing and legal steps handled.

Since 2019

Living & working in Cascais, the Golden Triangle

Who I am

My journey began in the competitive fashion industry in Israel, where I worked as a professional Buyer, learning to read quality, analyse market data and negotiate hard. In that world, "no" was always just the start of a deal.

Since moving to Cascais with my family in 2019, I have spent years immersed in the culture, the schools and the property market of the Golden Triangle. Today I represent both buyers and sellers, with the same sharp eye for value on either side.

That mix, a buyer's discipline and a resident's local knowledge, is what I bring to every client. Whether you're buying your first home in Portugal or selling one you've loved, I stay personally involved from the first conversation to the day the deed is signed.
